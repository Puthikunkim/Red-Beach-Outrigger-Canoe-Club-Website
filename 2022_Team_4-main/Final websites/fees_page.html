Fees Structures:

Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club Yearly Fees: $100

As a paid member of the Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club, your fees go towards:

  • Insurance of all club assets
  • Maintenance on all club owned canoes
  • Registration fees and maintenance fees opf all club trailers
  • Waka Trolley maintenance
  • Club Running Expenses
  • Affiliation fee for WANZ and AROCA

Nga Kaihoe O Aotearoa (WANZ or Waka Ama NZ)

WANZ is our governing National Body for Waka Ama, and the WANZ fee is an annaul fee payable per paddler per year to this Organisation. As an affiliated club, it is our responsibilitiy to collect the WANZ fee off each club member and pass it on to WANZ.

WANZ fees below:

WANZ Fee Structure

  • $15 per Open/Senior (20 years and over)
  • $12 per Junior (14 to 19 years)
  • $5 per Midget/Intermediate (13 years and under)

Auckland Region Outrigger Canoe Association (AROCA)

AROCA is the regional body for Waka Ama in Auckland and the Aroca fee is an annual fee payable per paddler per year to this organisation as of October 2016. As an affiliated club it is our responsibilitiy to collect the AROCA fee off each club member and pass it onto AROCA. AROCA Fees below:

AROCA Fee Structure:

  • $10 per Open/Senior (20 years and over)
  • $5 per Junior (14 to 19 years)
  • $5 per Midget/Intermediate (13 Years and under)

Note: Uniforms, regatta fees, canoe towing and your own paddle are in addition to this fee.
