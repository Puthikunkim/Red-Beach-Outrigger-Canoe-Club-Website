New Club Members:

Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club (RBOCC) has a trial period for all its “new paddlers” where they have 4 paddling sessions to decide whether or not they like the club and the sport of Waka Ama (Outrigger Canoe Paddling) before they are required to join the club to continue paddling. We allow the new members to get a taste of the sport before they continue as we would like to ensure they are committed towards our success as a group.

Existing Club Members

For existing club members club fees are due on the anniversary of their club membership to be paid via the clubs online portal. All membership payments are to be done through the clubs portal and any forms are to be returned to th club Treasurer. We are extremely greatful to all of our returning Waka Ama paddlers and are always looking to develop our relationship with our Whanau.

ID Cards:

WANZ ID registration cards have a fee of $5.00 per paddler. (These fees are subject to change, espesially at peak time, close to the Sprint Nationals where prices may increase). These cards are a must to be able to compete in any of the Waka Ama regattas. (No ID card, no paddle). An electronic copy of the items below must be sent to: info@rbocc.co.nz
